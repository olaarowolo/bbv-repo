Beautybyvickky Lounge Policies and Procedures

Beautybyvickky-lounge has specific policies and procedures for booking and attending sessions. Here’s a summary:

Booking Policy:

Client-Only Appointments: Appointments are exclusively for the scheduled client. Guests, including children, toddlers, and infants, are not permitted during the service.

Our Processes

Two-Step Process: The process typically involves two steps: an initial session followed by a perfecting session. While some clients may heal beautifully without a touch-up, most will need to return for a second session.

Possible Third Session: In rare cases, a third session may be required to achieve the desired look.

Perfecting Session Fee: The perfecting session incurs a separate fee and is not included with the initial session.

Touch-Up Session: Due to unpredictable results, it is highly recommended to book your 6-10 week touch-up session on the same day as your initial appointment. Although not required, it is your responsibility to schedule this session.

Booking Fee: A non-refundable booking fee of £50 is required when booking online to secure your appointment. This fee goes towards the total amount due on the day of the appointment, which must be paid in cash.

Non-Refundable Booking Fee: The booking fee is non-refundable; cancelling your appointment will result in the loss of this fee. It's advised to book wisely.

Age Requirement: You must be at least 18 years old to receive the procedure, even with parental consent.

Arrival Time: Arrive no later than 20 minutes before your scheduled appointment. Arriving late may result in a "no show" designation, leading to forfeiture of your booking fee. Consider traffic and weather conditions when planning your travel.

Rescheduling Notice: For rescheduling, a 48-hour notice is required via direct message or text. Less notice will require making another booking.

Foreign Retouches: All foreign retouches will be treated as a new set of brows, so please book accordingly.

Results Variability: Results will vary based on individual factors such as skin type, blood type, lifestyle, existing hair, and bone structure.

Previous Work Approval: If you have had previous work done by another artist, you must obtain approval before booking. Send clear photos of your full face and eyebrows with no makeup in natural lighting to the provided contact number for review. Booking without prior approval will lead to forfeiture of your deposit and cancellation of your appointment.

Price Changes: Prices are subject to change without notice, and the lounge reserves the right to refuse service to anyone.

For more details, you can visit the booking link provided on the Beautybyvickky-lounge website.

Pre-Care Instructions Before Your Procedure

Please follow these pre-care instructions to ensure the best results for your beauty procedure at Beautybyvickky Lounge:

DO NOT WORK OUT: Please refrain from exercising for 24 hours before your procedure.

AVOID ALCOHOL AND CAFFEINE: No alcohol or caffeine should be consumed 48 hours before the procedure. Note that decaf coffee and tea also contain caffeine.

AVOID SUN AND TANNING: Stay out of the sun and avoid tanning for one week prior to your procedure.

MEDICATIONS TO AVOID: Do not take aspirin, niacin, vitamin E, or Advil/Ibuprofen for 48 hours before the procedure.

SUPPLEMENTS TO AVOID: Refrain from fish oil, prenatal vitamins, nutritional shakes (like Shakeology), and "hair, skin, nail" supplements 48 hours prior to the procedure.

RETIN-A: Discontinue the use of Retin-A at least 2 weeks prior to your appointment and avoid it on the eyebrow area after the procedure.

ALPHA HYDROXY ACID (AHA) PRODUCTS: Avoid using any AHA products close to the eyebrow area for 2 weeks prior to the procedure, and refrain from using them on the eyebrow area after the procedure.

BOTOX AND FILLER: Botox and filler injections in the forehead, temple, and eye area should be avoided for 1 month prior to your procedure.

BROW WAXING, TINTING, OR THREADING: Do not wax, tint, or thread your brows one week prior to the procedure. The more natural hair growth you have, the better the results!

CHEMICAL PEELS AND FACIALS: Avoid chemical peels, microdermabrasion, or facials for 4 weeks prior to your procedure.

Following these instructions can help ensure optimal results and a smoother experience during your appointment at Beautybyvickky Lounge. For more details, please check the information provided during your booking process.

Aftercare Instructions for Semipermanent Brows

To ensure optimal healing and results for your semipermanent brows, it is crucial to follow these aftercare instructions:

  1. Keep the area clean: It is important to keep the treated area clean to help prevent infection and ensure proper healing. Gently clean the area with mild soap and water a few times a day.
  2. Avoid picking or scratching: Do not pick or scratch at the treated area, as this can lead to infection and may cause the pigment to fade or become uneven.
  3. Avoid direct sun exposure: Avoid exposing the treated area to direct sunlight or tanning beds for at least two weeks after the procedure. Sun exposure can cause the pigment to fade or become discolored.
  4. Protect the area with ointment or cream: We recommend using a specific ointment or cream to keep the area moisturized and protected.
  5. Avoid strenuous activities: Avoid activities that may cause sweating or irritation to the treated area, such as exercise or hot showers, for at least a few days after the procedure.
  6. Avoid makeup: Avoid wearing makeup on the treated area for at least a few days after the procedure. If you must wear makeup, be sure to use a gentle, oil-free formula and avoid rubbing or scratching the area.

It is important to follow these specific aftercare instructions to ensure the best possible healing and results. If you have any questions or concerns about the aftercare process, be sure to ask your practitioner for more guidance
